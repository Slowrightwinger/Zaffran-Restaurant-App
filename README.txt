# Zaffran Restaurant App

## Overview
Zaffran is a restaurant app designed to provide a seamless dining experience. The app allows users to view the menu, place orders, and make reservations at Zaffran restaurant. Built with a focus on user-friendliness and efficiency, Zaffran aims to enhance the dining experience for our customers.

## Features
- **View Menu**: Browse the restaurant's menu with detailed descriptions and images.
- **Place Orders**: Order your favorite dishes directly from the app.
- **Make Reservations**: Reserve a table at Zaffran with ease.
- **User Authentication**: Secure login and registration for personalized experiences.
- **Real-time Notifications**: Receive updates on your order status and reservation confirmations.
